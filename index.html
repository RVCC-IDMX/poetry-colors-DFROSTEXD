The Poem

Upon the hill, the castle stands,
A beacon bright in distant lands.
The moonlight casts its silver glow,
On tales of yore, from long ago.

The bard’s soft lute, a gentle sound,
Echoes through the halls profound.
In every stone, a story’s told,
Of knights so brave and hearts of gold.

About This Poem

This poem captures the essence of medieval life, with its castles, knights, and bards. The website design reflects the poem’s theme, transporting readers to a bygone era.
